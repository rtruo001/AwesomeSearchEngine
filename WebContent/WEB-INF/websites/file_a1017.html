Seating is general admission for all sports except Men's Basketball.  

Online sales are available for purchase until 3 hours before the start of the game with some exceptions.

UC Riverside undergraduate students are free at regular season home games by showing their UCR ID at the game to pick up their student ticket.

Ticket sales open one hour prior to the game at the venue with the exception of baseball which opens 90 minutes prior to the start of the game.

The Athletics Ticket Office is located in the Athletics & Dance Building, room 117. Hours vary due to game days and meetings, please contact the Athletics Ticket Office at (951) 827-4653 or highlandertickets@ucr.edu to pick up your tickets.
