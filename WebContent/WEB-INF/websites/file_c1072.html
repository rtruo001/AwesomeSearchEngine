Enrollment and Academic Forms

Last Updated: April 3, 2014 10:24:01 AM PDT
Give feedback

Find links to frequently used enrollment and academic forms.

Please make additional copies for necessary distribution.
Form Additional information
Clerical Error Form (PDF) Complete this form to initiate a grade change caused by a clerical or procedural error.
College Action Notification Form (E-form) Complete this form when requesting that actions be taken on a student’s record.
Course Approval Form (PDF) Complete this form when requesting course approval.
Double Major Petition (PDF) Complete this form when requesting approval to declare a double major. See more information about double major petitions and when to use this form.
Graduate Degree and Diploma Application (PDF) Complete this form to apply for a diploma.
Graduate Student General Petition Form Please see your department coordinator for this form.
Incomplete (PDF) Complete this form when requesting an Incomplete (I) grade.
Minor declaration (PDF) This form is for exceptions and summer graduates only. It should not be used in place of the online tool. See more information about minor declaration and when to use this form.
Petition for Undergraduate Credit by Examination (PDF) Complete this form when requesting credit by examination.
Undergraduate Student Petition (PDF) If Undergraduate Withdrawal e-form is not sufficient, college staff should contact Academic Records for special circumstances.
Undergraduate Withdrawal Form (E-Form) Make sure students review How to Withdraw from UCSD and advise them to use the E-form link on that page, if all requirements are met.
For more information, contact Academic Records at (858) 534-3144.
