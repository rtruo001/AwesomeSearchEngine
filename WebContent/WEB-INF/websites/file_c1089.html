Planned Downtime- UC Learning System Update

Status: open

Incident report: The University of California is updating the UC Learning Center, the training and professional development system we offer to all UC San Diego staff and faculty. The system will be unavailable January 29, 2015 at 5pm (PST) until Wednesday, February 4.

Beginning February 4, when you log in to the UC Learning Center, you will note that the look and feel has been updated, internet browser compatibility has been improved, and new features have been added. Please note that:

• The system will be unavailable January 29, 2015 at 5pm (PST) until Wednesday, February 4.
• Your current registrations and completions will not be impacted.
• Any in-person training scheduled during this time will take place.
• Additional communication will be issued to familiarize you with the updated system

Contact for this incident report: lms-support@ucsd.edu
