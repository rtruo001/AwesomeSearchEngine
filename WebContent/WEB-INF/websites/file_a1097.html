UCR is the UC of Opportunity

UCR has received approval for both a New School of Medicine and a School of Public Policy.  And, the Department of Ethnic Studies will become only the third school in the nation to offer a doctoral degree in Ethnic Studies, while the Anderson Graduate School of Management (AGSM) and the Garduate School of Education (GSOE) both have added four new faculty members this fall to broaden their offerings and provide new opportunties for student and faculty alike.
