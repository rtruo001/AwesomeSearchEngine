What we do

OCGA is responsible for the stewardship of sponsored research and research collaborations from government (federal and state), for-profit industry, non-profit, foreign, and University of California sponsors. We:

  • Review and submit proposals to sponsors.
  • Analyze and negotiate contract and grant award terms and conditions to ensure compliance with sponsor terms and conditions, university policies, and federal regulations and requirements.
  • Administrate post-award re-negotiation, modification, and problem resolution.
  • Negotiate and sign unfunded agreements such as material transfers, memorandum of understanding, and confidential/non-disclosure agreements.
  • Negotiate and execute outgoing subawards and subawards to other UC campuses (multi-campus agreements).
  • Facilitate issuance of UCSD-provided service agreements.
