New Student Orientations

Each of UC San Diego's six colleges presents a mandatory orientation program for incoming freshmen, transfer students and parents. International students have an additional orientation session at the International Center.

Orientation sessions help you:

  • Understand your college and its requirements
  • Meet other new students
  • Learn about important campus resources

Check your college's website (in the spring) for dates, costs and how to sign up for the programs.

Note: You will not enroll in classes during orientation. Enrollment occurs later, in a separate online process on WebReg. You can review the WebReg tutorial from the Registrar's Office.

Contact an Admissions Officer assigned to your area.
