Health Sciences Sponsored Project Pre-Award Office

Mission Statement

The Health Sciences Sponsored Project Pre-Award Office (HS SPPO) exists to provide exceptional customer service and guidance training to faculty, students and staff to ensure they submit superior quality proposals that maximize extramural funding for the Health Sciences (HS) community, as well as further their personal and professional advancement.

 

Our Role and Responsibilities

Pre-award office for NIH & AHRQ and NIH & AHRQ subaward proposals for Health Sciences: Review, sign and submit Health Sciences proposals for NIH and AHRQ funding. Review and sign and submit Just-in-Time documents for NIH, AHRQ and related subawards, and most other actions required by the funding agency prior to the release of an award, with the exception of advance approval to spend requests.

PI exception requests: review of all PI exception requests for Health Sciences proposals and prospective PIs, regardless of funding agency, on behalf of the Associate Dean for Academic Affairs, Health Sciences - School of Medicine and School of Pharmacy and Pharmaceutical Sciences.

Health Sciences Fellowships, Scholarships and Loan Repayment Program award applications: Review and institutional signature for fellowship, scholarship and Loan Repayment Program award applications and acceptance documents for Health Sciences fellows and faculty for any applications and awards which are not handled as grant proposals by OCGA (Office of Contract & Grant Administration).
