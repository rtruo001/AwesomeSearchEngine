Driving Directions to the Center

UCR Palm Desert is located at 75080 Frank Sinatra Drive at Cook Street.

To enter at the Berger Drive Entrance:
From Interstate 10 Eastbound or Westbound
  • Exit at Cook Street.
  • Proceed south on Cook; turning left on Berger Drive (same entrance to CSUSB-Palm Desert Campus).
  • Travel along Berger Drive past the CSUSB-Palm Desert Campus.
  • Turn right at the monument sign that says "UCR Palm Desert" into Parking Lot B.
From Highway 111
  • Proceed north on Cook Street; turning right on Frank Sinatra Drive.
  • Turn left on Berger Drive.
  • Turn left at the monument sign that says "UCR Palm Desert" into Parking Lot B.
     

UCR Palm Desert Center Parking Information

Parking permits are required at UCR Palm Desert 24 hours-a-day, 365 days-a-year. Purchase daily permits in Parking Lot B. Learn more about parking and ways to purchase permits.
