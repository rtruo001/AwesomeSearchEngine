Research: Preparing Proposals

Last Updated: February 4, 2015 2:02:18 PM PST
Give feedback

Find resources and links for collecting general information as first steps in preparing proposals.

  • Proposal Development - Find links to specific information to be included in proposals.
  • Research Administrative Offices - A list of central research administrative offices at UC San Diego.
  • Research Equipment Resources - A list of current research facilities that are accessible to UCSD faculty, fellows, staff, and students.
  • Clinical Research and Trials - Links to information on PI-initiated clinical trials.
  • Compliance Requirements - Links to information on research compliance requirements.
  • Data Management Plans - Links and information to required Data Management Plans in Research Cyberinfrastructure project.
  • Working with Sponsors -  Links to information about working with sponsors.
  • Types of Sponsored Research Activity - Find out what types of awards UC San Diego receives and who to contact for assistance in pre-award administration.
  • OCGA Forms - Find links to proposal and award forms.
